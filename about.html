Inspiration:

Our inspiration was Zynga and Google Maps.

What it does:

Our goal with MapsVR is to help people with physical disabilities navigate the insides of buildings easily.

How we built it:

We used A-Frame combined with Javascript, HTML/CSS to build this webapp.

Challenges we ran into:

We had minor issues connecting our CSS page to our HTML and downloading our images of Zynga for A-Frame.

Accomplishments that we're proud of:

We're proud that we successfully coded our own virtual reality application that can be useful for anyone- whether they are lost or have a disability.

What we learned:

We learned how to use A-Frame to create a realistic model of the inside of Zynga's headquarters.

What's next for MapsVR:

MapsVR has the potential to branch out to mapping typical public places such as restaurants, parks, public restrooms and transportation methods such as BART, buses, and airports.
